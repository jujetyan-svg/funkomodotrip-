Itinerary (Approximate)

05:30 – 06:00 | Pickup at hotel and transfer to harbour (Labuan Bajo) + briefing.

06:00 – 07:00 | Travel to Padar Island.

07:00 – 08:30 | Trekking on Padar Island viewpoint.

08:30 – 09:00 | Depart for Pink Beach.

09:00 – 10:00 | Relax & snorkel at Pink Beach; lunch box & snack served.

10:00 – 11:00 | Depart to Komodo Island.

11:00 – 12:00 | Explore Komodo Island with ranger.

12:00 – 12:30 | Travel to Taka Makassar (Sandbank).

12:30 – 13:30 | Relax & photo session at Taka Makassar.

13:30 – 14:30 | Snorkeling at Manta Point.

14:30 – 15:30 | Snorkeling at Turtle Point.

15:30 – 17:00 | Return to Labuan Bajo harbour and transfer back to hotel.
