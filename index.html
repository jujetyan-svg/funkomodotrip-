3D/2N Komodo Adventure Package

Day 1: Labuan Bajo Explorer

  • Morning: Pickup from Labuan Bajo to Kelor Island for trekking and swimming to see baby sharks
  • Afternoon: Sailing to Manjarite for snorkeling to see coral reefs and various kinds of fish
  • Evening: Visit Kalong Island to watch thousands of bats flying and enjoy the sunset

Day 2: Island Hopping

  • Early Morning: Trekking to see sunrise from Padar Island, then head to Pink Beach for beach time and snorkeling
  • Afternoon: Sailing to Komodo Island to watch wildlife and Komodo dragons, then Manta Point to swim with manta rays
  • Evening: Visit Taka Makasar to see a small sandbank beach

Day 3: Underwater Life

  • Morning: Snorkeling at Sebayur to see various fish
  • Afternoon: Return to Labuan Bajo (optional flight at 14:00)
